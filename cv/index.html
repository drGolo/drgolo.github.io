Summary Dr Golo Henseke is an Associate Professor at the UCL Institute of Education. His research focuses on skills, labour markets, technological change, subjective well-being and quantitative measurement methods. He has expertise in designing and managing large survey datasets, conducting complex statistical analysis, and leading large-scale research projects. His work is published in Oxford Economic Papers, ILR Review, British Journal of Industrial Relations, and New Technology, Work and Employment.
